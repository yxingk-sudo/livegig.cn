### 1. 合并日期列

#### 表头修改
```html

<th><i class="bi bi-calendar-x me-1"></i>退房日期</th>

<th><i class="bi bi-calendar-range me-1"></i>住宿日期</th>
```

#### 数据单元格修改
```php
<!-- 优化前 -->
<td>
    <span class="badge bg-info bg-opacity-10 text-info">
        <i class="bi bi-calendar-event me-1"></i>2024-01-01
    </span>
</td>
<td>
    <span class="badge bg-info bg-opacity-10 text-info">
        <i class="bi bi-calendar-event me-1"></i>2024-01-05
    </span>
</td>

<!-- 优化后 -->
<td>
    <span class="fw-semibold">
        <i class="bi bi-calendar-event text-primary me-1"></i>2024-01-01
        <span class="text-muted mx-2">至</span>
        <i class="bi bi-calendar-check text-success me-1"></i>2024-01-05
    </span>
</td>
```

#### 显示逻辑
- ✅ **完整日期范围**：同时显示入住和退房日期，中间用"至"连接
- ✅ **仅入住日期**：显示入住日期 + "起"
- ✅ **仅退房日期**：显示退房日期 + "止"
- ✅ **无日期**：显示 "-"
